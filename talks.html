Dissertation Presentation, Oxford University "Mathematics are not broken" March 2021

Presentation associated to my mathematical dissertation on Random Fractals, in the context of my master in mathematical sciences at Oxford.
The object of interest is a certain type of random fractals called percolations. We study the probability that a crossing of the percolation exists. [All numerical results presented agree with the mathematical theory, hence the title]
