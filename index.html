Course Notes

I consider myself as an "open source mathematician";
As well as open source programmers, I think that any knowledge should be shared.
Therefore, I share my hand-written notes from lectures.
1st year
Introduction:
  • Bridging course
  • Introduction to "University Mathematics"
1st term:
  • Analysis "1"(Study of Sequences, Continuity and Differentiability)
  • Algebra "1"(Introduction to Logic, Set Theory, Functions and Mappings, Permutations, Fields)
  • Applied Mathematics
  • Mathematical Methods "1"(Basic Calculus)
2nd term:
  • Analysis "2"(Series and Integration)
  • Algebra "2"(Introduction to Number Theory, Groups and Linear Algebra)
  • Newtonian Mechanics
  • Mathematical Methods "2"(Theorems of Green and Stokes)
2nd year
1st term:
  • Complex Analysis
  • Linear Algebra
  • Fluids Mechanics
  • Mathematical Methods "3"(Calculus of Variations, and Partial Differential Equations)
2nd term:
  • Real Analysis
  • Groups and Rings Algebra
  • Number Theory
  • Mathematical Methods "4"(Further Partial Differential Equations)
  • Probabilities and Statistics *
3rd year
1st term:
  • Multi-variable Analysis
  • Measure Theory
  • Galois Theory
  • Elliptic Curves
  • Commutative Algebra *
  • Differential Geometry *
2nd term:
  • Functional Analysis
  • Graph Theory and Combinatorics
  • Algebraic Number Theory
  • Probability
  • Mathematical Logic *
